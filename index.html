About Me

A Few Words About Me

Born and raised in Quang Ngai, I am currently a student at Duy Tan University in Da Nang. Since childhood, I have had a deep passion and curiosity for technology and electronic devices. In my free time, I often spend time exploring and researching them, which has helped me accumulate some knowledge along the way.

A Turning Point in Life

The real turning point came during my high school years, especially during the COVID-19 pandemic. With more time at home, I started learning web development and exploring everything that sparked my curiosity. Fortunately, I received generous guidance and support from experienced mentors, which allowed me to absorb a wealth of valuable knowledge. Beyond just learning, I also built a few small websites on my own, marking important milestones in my personal growth journey.

Hobbies

Besides programming, I have many other interests. I love playing football with my friends, both for fun and to stay active. Additionally, I have a passion for exploring new technologies and discovering unique things I haven't encountered before. I also enjoy meeting and chatting with friends to expand my network and learn interesting things from others. Another favorite hobby of mine is sightseeing, especially visiting places with pristine and peaceful nature.

Strengths and Weaknesses

Regarding my strengths, I am naturally curious and always eager to explore and learn about everything around me. Once I find something interesting, I dedicate myself to researching and working hard on it. This trait motivates me to continuously learn and develop myself. Additionally, I am quite open and enjoy socializing with others, which helps me expand my network and gain valuable knowledge from those around me.

However, I also have weaknesses that I am working to improve. I can be quite lazy at times and easily lose focus when working on a specific task. Sometimes, I fall into a state of “VIRTUAL EFFORT”—feeling like I’ve done a lot, but in reality, not achieving the desired results. Nevertheless, I am continuously striving to improve myself and find ways to overcome these weaknesses to become better in the future.
